Rizzmatch AI — Account Deletion Request

You can request the deletion of your Rizzmatch AI account and all associated data at any time. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent. Once deleted, your profile, photos, matches, and messages cannot be recovered.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile (name, photos, bio, preferences)
  • All matches and conversations
  • Your account credentials
  • Location and device data

What may be retained

  • Safety reports and moderation records (as required by law)
  • Transaction records (as required by Indian tax regulations)

Retained data is deleted within 90 days unless legally required to be kept longer.

How to request deletion

Send an email from the address associated with your Rizzmatch AI account to:

Request Account Deletion

Include your registered phone number or email in the message. We will process your request within 30 days and send a confirmation.
