Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

Rizzmatch ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application Rizzmatch ("App"). Rizzmatch is a dating app intended exclusively for users aged 18 and above.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Name, phone number, email address, date of birth, and gender.
  • Profile information: Photos, bio, occupation, city, relationship intent, religion, languages, and lifestyle preferences you choose to share.
  • Verification data: Selfie photo submitted for identity verification. This is used solely to verify that your profile photo matches your real appearance and is not shared with other users.
  • Location data: We collect your approximate location (city level) to show relevant matches nearby. We do not track your precise location in the background.
  • Usage data: Swipe activity, matches, messages, and interaction history within the app.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system, and push notification tokens.
  • Payment information: Subscription payment data is processed by Razorpay. We do not store your card or UPI details.
  • Safety reports: If you report or block another user, we collect the details of your report to review and take action.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To show you potential matches based on your preferences and location.
  • To enable messaging between matched users.
  • To send push notifications about matches and messages.
  • To process subscription payments.
  • To verify user identity and maintain profile authenticity.
  • To review reports, investigate safety concerns, and enforce our Terms of Service.
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent, abusive, or illegal activity.
  • To improve and personalize the App experience.

3. Information We Share

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in the following cases:

  • Other users: Your profile (photos, name, bio, city, and preferences you have set to visible) is shown to other users on the platform. Private information such as your phone number and email is never shared.
  • Service providers: We use Firebase (Google) for authentication and cloud services, Razorpay for payments, and cloud infrastructure providers to operate the App.
  • Safety and legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the safety and rights of our users or the public. We cooperate with law enforcement when legally required.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Used for authentication and backend services. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In: If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from your Google account. We do not receive your Google password. Google Privacy Policy
  • Razorpay: Used for processing payments. Razorpay Privacy Policy

5. Safety Features

Rizzmatch provides built-in tools to keep you safe:

  • Block: You can block any user at any time. A blocked user will no longer appear in your discover feed and cannot contact you.
  • Report: You can report any profile for spam, inappropriate content, fake profiles, harassment, or other concerns. Our team reviews all reports and takes action, which may include warnings, suspensions, or permanent bans.
  • Profile verification: Users can verify their identity through a selfie check, helping ensure profiles are real people.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request account deletion at any time by contacting us or using the delete option within the App. Upon deletion, your profile, photos, and messages will be permanently removed within 30 days. Safety reports and moderation records may be retained for longer periods as required by law or for platform safety purposes.

7. Your Rights

  • Access and update your profile information within the App.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Opt out of push notifications via your device settings.
  • Withdraw location permission via your device settings at any time.
  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you by contacting us.

8. Children's Privacy

Rizzmatch is strictly intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a user is under 18, their account will be immediately and permanently terminated and their data deleted. If you believe a minor is using our App, please contact us immediately at support.rizzmatch@gmail.com.

9. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), Firebase Authentication with secure token handling, and secure cloud storage. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we encourage you to protect your account credentials.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or via email. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
