Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

Rizzmatch ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application Rizzmatch ("App").

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Name, phone number, email address, date of birth, and gender.
  • Profile information: Photos, bio, occupation, city, and relationship preferences you choose to share.
  • Location data: We collect your approximate location (city level) to show relevant matches nearby. We do not track your precise location in the background.
  • Usage data: Swipe activity, matches, and messages within the app.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system, and push notification tokens.
  • Payment information: Subscription payment data is processed by Razorpay. We do not store your card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To show you potential matches based on your preferences and location.
  • To enable messaging between matched users.
  • To send push notifications about matches and messages.
  • To process subscription payments.
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent or abusive activity.
  • To improve and personalize the App experience.

3. Information We Share

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in the following cases:

  • Other users: Your profile (photos, name, bio, city) is visible to other users on the platform.
  • Service providers: We use Firebase (Google) for authentication and cloud services, Razorpay for payments, and cloud infrastructure providers to operate the App.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Used for authentication and backend services. Google Privacy Policy
  • Razorpay: Used for processing payments. Razorpay Privacy Policy

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request account deletion at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, your profile, photos, and messages will be permanently removed within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

  • Access and update your profile information within the App.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Opt out of push notifications via your device settings.
  • Withdraw location permission via your device settings at any time.

7. Children's Privacy

Rizzmatch is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a user is under 18, their account will be immediately terminated.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), Firebase Authentication, and secure cloud storage. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
